About Me

Who Am I

I don't learn things in a vacuum. I learn by building.

I'm driven by solving real problems for real people not grades, not recognition. That's what motivates me. The AI Calendar Suite came from watching students struggle with time management. Botler came from noticing my own time management could be smarter with systems. Both came from identifying a friction point and building a solution.

Multidisciplinary rigor isn't just a phrase, it's how I work. I move between full-stack web development, machine learning, data analysis, cybersecurity, and game theory. I study R for statistical modeling and actuarial science. I participate in competitions like Cyberthon. I taught web development to my peers.
